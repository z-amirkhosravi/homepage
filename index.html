About

My name is Zavosh. I'm a Canadian living in Toronto. Here are some random facts about me:

  • I have a PhD in number theory from the University of Toronto. Here is a link to my math genealogy page. According to that site I'm a mathematical descendant of both Gauss and Euler.

  • I've been a coder longer than I've been a mathematician -- since the days when DOS 3.3 was the standard operating system on PCs, and the state of the art in computer graphics was Mode X of the VGA card.

  • I'm fluent in Persian, and once knew French and German at a B1 level. My German has since decayed, but my French has improved. I also studied Latin and Ancient Greek at university -- one of my favorite Latin poems is Catullus 85.
